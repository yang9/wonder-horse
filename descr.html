A BRIEF HISTORY

A clydesdale gestation period is 11 months.

"Babies are about 3 feet tall at the shoulders at birth and weigh 125 pounds.

Adult Clydesdales are 6 feet tall at the shoulder and usually

weigh between 1,600 and 2,200 pounds.

Most Clydesdales live to 20-25 years of age".

"Most of the babies are born late at night", says Norm Wilke of Bargin Barn Warehouse in Shiloh, Missouri.*

They are known as the "GENTLE GIANTS" in the horse world.
